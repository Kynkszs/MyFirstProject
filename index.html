About us

Starr's Detailing is a locally owned and operated car detailing business in Creston, Iowa, dedicated to keeping your vehicle looking its absolute best. Whether it's a quick wash or a full interior and exterior detail, Starr's combines precision, care, and high-quality products to restore that showroom shine. Known for friendly service and attention to detail, the team takes pride in serving the Creston community with affordable packages that fit every driver’s needs—from daily commuters to weekend cruisers. With flexible scheduling, mobile services, and a passion for clean rides, Starr's Detailing is where Creston goes for a spotless finish and genuine small-town service.
